This Software Process Book is a collection of best practice knowledge captured from both 1060 Research and community collaboration.
It's scope is broad - to document knowledge of how to architect, design, implement, test, tune, take to production, manage and maintain
systems developed with 1060 NetKernel using the Resource Oriented Computing (ROC) approach.

We welcome your input. Everything from typos, corrections and suggestions to ownership and writing of pages or complete sections.
